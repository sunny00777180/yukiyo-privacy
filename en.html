Privacy Policy

JittaKey – Japanese Input Keyboard

Last Updated: June 18, 2025

1. Personal Information Collected

This app does not collect any personal information such as typed content, keystroke records, contacts, or location data.
Only app settings (alphabet/small gana display ON/OFF) are saved locally on the user's device and are not transmitted externally.

2. Full Access Request

YUKIYO Keyboard may request “Full Access” to enable typing sounds and haptic feedback.
However, input data is never collected or transmitted externally.

3. Third-party Sharing

No information is shared with third parties or transmitted to external servers.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

YUKIYO does not store personal data on servers. All local settings are deleted when the app is removed.

5. User Rights

Users may delete or reset the app at any time. For inquiries, please contact us via the email below.
